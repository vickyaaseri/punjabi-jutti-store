About Us

Welcome to Punjabi Jutti Store, where tradition meets elegance.

Our juttis are handcrafted by skilled artisans, ensuring quality and authenticity that reflect the rich heritage of Punjabi footwear. With a 100-year legacy of craftsmanship passed down through generations, we bring you footwear that embodies tradition and style.

  • Handcrafted with Love: Each pair is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans, ensuring attention to detail and exceptional quality.
  • Real Leather: Made from premium-quality leather, our juttis offer superior comfort, durability, and breathability.
  • Timeless Designs: Explore a diverse range of traditional and contemporary designs, from classic embroidered juttis to modern creations.
  • Comfortable Fit: Designed for comfort and flexibility, our juttis ensure all-day wearability.
  • 1-Year Guarantee: We stand behind our craftsmanship with a 1-Year Guarantee on made-to-order juttis.
  • Sustainable Practices: Committed to ethical sourcing and sustainability, we ensure minimal environmental impact.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our priority is providing an excellent shopping experience and unparalleled customer service.

Join us in celebrating the art of traditional footwear. Explore our collection and find the perfect pair of juttis to elevate your style.
