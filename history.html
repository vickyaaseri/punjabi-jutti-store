Our History

The Legacy of Our Handcrafted Leather Juttis
Our journey began in 1925 in a humble workshop in Sayad Wala, near Abohar in Punjab’s Fazilka district. Passed down through three generations, our family business is dedicated to crafting exquisite juttis that blend durability with elegance. Inspired by Punjab’s rich cultural heritage, our artisans have preserved traditional craftsmanship while embracing contemporary trends.

Craftsmanship & Quality
Every jutti is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans who take pride in their work. By merging traditional techniques with modern designs, we ensure unmatched quality and attention to detail. Our commitment to customization was reinforced when we crafted a bespoke pair for a bride seeking the perfect fit—a philosophy we continue to uphold.

Premium Materials & Sustainability
We source only the finest leather, ensuring durability, comfort, and a luxurious feel. Our commitment to sustainability means prioritizing eco-friendly materials and supporting local artisans, helping us minimize environmental impact while maintaining superior craftsmanship.

Customer Satisfaction
Our priority is delivering an exceptional shopping experience. From personalized service to a diverse collection of styles, our juttis offer the perfect blend of comfort and style—ideal for everyday wear, festive occasions, or statement pieces.
